Welcome to Pune Vidyarthi Griha's College Engineering and Management Institute


About Us

Established in 1985, Pune Vidhyarthi Griha’s College of Engineering and Technology and G K Pate(Wani) Institute of Management, an affiliate to the Savitribai Phule Pune University and accredited ‘A’ by NAAC is one of the reputed engineering colleges in India. College provides courses on Engineering disciplines and offers some of the most advanced platforms and infrastructure to its students.




Branches

Computer Engineering

Computer Engineering Department was established in the year 2002 with undergraduate course. The Department has sanctioned intake of 60 students. The department has qualified, experienced and dedicated teaching staff all having PG qualification. Department is well equipped with fully developed laboratories with equipment having internet facility in each laboratory as per the requirements of SPPU and AICTE. Department emphasizes on effective teaching learning process with excellent teaching learning environment. The department has good association with eminent persons from both industry and academics.


Electrical Engineering

The Department of Electrical Engineering aims to impart quality education for developing Electrical Engineers to cater the needs of society and mankind. The department observes ethical practices in teaching-learning and research. Department has a team of well qualified, highly experienced, dedicated and motivated faculty members. Our faculty members are involved in research in upcoming modern areas of electrical engineering such as Electric Vehicles, Renewable Energy Systems, Robotics, Artificial Intelligence techniques etc.




Electronics Engineering

The department of Electronics and Telecommunication engineering established in the year 1991 with undergraduate course. It has qualified, experienced and dedicated teaching staff. The department emphasizes on effective teaching learning processes with excellent teaching learning environment. There are total 21 Teaching Staff and 5 non-teaching staff. The Department emphasizes an active and blended project/problem based learning approach for effective outcome based education with an excellent teaching learning environment.



AI & DS

Keeping pace with the ever-changing context of technology, visionary leadership of PVGCOET & GKPIM, Pune has decided to introduce a full time, four year under graduate course specializing in Artificial Intelligence and Data Sciences (AI & DS) from Academic Year 2021-2022 with the intake capacity of 60 seats. The Department will be fully functional from A.Y. 2022-2023.



Mechanical Engineering

The Department of Mechanical Engineering was established in the year 1991 with an intake of 60 students, which was increased to 120 students in the year 2009. Since its inception , the Department has been offering a four-year B.E. program in Mechanical Engineering at undergraduate level while at the postgraduate level, it offers Ph.D. program in Mechanical Engineering.



Printing Engineering

The department was established in the year 1985 by our Founder, Principal and Director Dr. Pa. Bha. Kulkarni. Started as a small department with strength of 30 students has now a developed into a flourished department with 30 batches passed out till date. The curriculum covers all aspects of graphic design, print media and packaging along with interdisciplinary courses like computer, mechanical, electrical, electronics and information technology.



Information Technology

Welcome to the department of Information Technology. The Web has revolutionized in the past 50 years. Exchange of Information anytime, anywhere is the vital part of today’s world. Technologies like Big data, Cloud Computing, Internet of Thingsare emerged. Information Technology is the backbone of all these innovations. The aim of the department is to impart quality education through innovative teaching learning. The program provides broad spectrum of courses, internship programs, projects. Students get enriched through development programs, workshops, audit courses. Our enthusiastic faculty take lot of efforts to make our students competent.
